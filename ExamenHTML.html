The Importance of Learning HTML

In today's digital era, HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is an essential skill for web developers. Without understanding HTML, creating a professional website is nearly impossible. However, once you master it, the possibilities are limitless.

Why Should You Learn HTML?

"HTML is the foundation of the web." - Tim Berners-Lee

HTML is a language that allows you to structure your web content effectively. It is not only about how your content looks, but also how accessible it is.
